## Purpose

Measure system health, not user behavior.
Flip the Switch does not track people to control them.

---

## What We Measure (Allowed)

- Intake completion rate
- Time mode selection frequency
- Skip frequency (system tuning)
- Drop-off points (UX refinement)
- Feature usage (aggregate only)

---

## What We Do NOT Measure (Banned)

- Emotional states as metrics
- "Success scores"
- Behavioral scoring
- Psychological profiling
- Any ranking of users

---

## Core Metric

**Did the system reduce friction and increase clarity?**
