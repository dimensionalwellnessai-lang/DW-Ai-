# App Store Reviewer Notes

## App Category
Health & Fitness / Lifestyle

## Core Description
Flip the Switch is a calm, consent-based AI assistant that helps users build their own life system through adaptive guidance.

## Important Disclosures

1. **Not Medical Advice**
   - This app does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment
   - This app is not a substitute for professional healthcare
   - No claims of curing or treating any condition

2. **User Control**
   - All actions are user-controlled and consent-based
   - Users explicitly confirm any saves or schedule additions
   - No auto-scheduling or forced routines

3. **Data Handling**
   - User data is stored securely
   - Users can delete their data at any time
   - No selling of user data to third parties

4. **AI Features**
   - AI provides supportive guidance, not therapy
   - AI responses are transparent about adjustments
   - No manipulation or dark patterns
